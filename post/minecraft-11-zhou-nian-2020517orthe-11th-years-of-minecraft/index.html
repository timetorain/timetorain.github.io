Minecraft 11周年 2020.5.17|The 11th years of Minecraft

2020-05-13

Minecraft在不知不觉中已陪伴我们走过十一年，今天是5月13日，离第一个有关Minecraft的视频发布已过去了十一年，或许5月17日才是Minecraft真正发布的日子，但是我仍然在此提前恭贺Minecraft:"十一周年快乐!"
——致那些逝去的曾经
Minecraft has been with us for eleven years without knowing it. Today is May 13th. It has been 11 years since the first video about Minecraft was released. Perhaps May 17th is the day when Minecraft was really released , But I am still here to congratulate Minecraft in advance: "Happy 11th Anniversary!"
——To those who have passed away
